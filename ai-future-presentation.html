Three Scenarios for 2035
Which world are we building?
SCENARIO A
Acceleration
≈ 25% likely

Open models + cheap compute compound. AI becomes a utility layer beneath every product.

  • +15% global GDP by 2035
  • Universal AI tutors & doctors
  • Massive labor reallocation
$15T annual value creation
SCENARIO B
Equilibrium
≈ 55% likely

Regulation, capability, and trust scale together. Steady but uneven diffusion across sectors.

  • AI co-pilots standard at work
  • Strong EU/US/Asia governance regimes
  • Productivity +1.5% / yr
Most likely path
SCENARIO C
Friction
≈ 20% likely

Safety incidents, compute bottlenecks, or geopolitics fragment global AI development.

  • Splintered model ecosystems
  • Trust collapse in synthetic media
  • Innovation drag in regulated sectors
−$3T opportunity cost
